# Wintergreen Color Palette

A dark, cool-toned theme inspired by winter frost and evergreen forests.

## Core Colors

### Backgrounds

- **Base**: #1a2332 (Main background - dark slate blue)
- **Mantle**: #151d28 (Slightly darker variant)
- **Crust**: #0f1419 (Darkest variant)
- **Surface0**: #243342 (Elevated surfaces)
- **Surface1**: #2d3f52 (More elevated)
- **Surface2**: #364b62 (Highest elevation)

### Foregrounds

- **Text**: #c8d5e3 (Main text - soft blue-white)
- **Subtext1**: #a8b8c7 (Secondary text)
- **Subtext0**: #95a7b8 (Tertiary text)

### Overlays

- **Overlay0**: #495f75 (Borders/dividers)
- **Overlay1**: #5a6f85 (Inactive elements)
- **Overlay2**: #6b8095 (Comments/subtle text)

## Accent Colors

### Primary Accents (Wintergreen Theme)

- **Teal**: #73c5b8 (Primary accent - wintergreen)
- **Wintermint**: #81d4be (Brighter mint)
- **Frost**: #99e0d9 (Ice/frost highlights)
- **Pine**: #5f9985 (Deep evergreen)
- **Evergreen**: #4a8570 (Darkest green)

### Cool Tones

- **Sky**: #7dcfe6 (Light cyan)
- **Sapphire**: #6db8d6 (Medium blue)
- **Blue**: #7da6d6 (Functions/methods)
- **Lavender**: #a5b8e6 (Operators/special)

### Warm Accents

- **Green**: #8fc5a1 (Success/strings)
- **Yellow**: #e6d89c (Warnings/types)
- **Peach**: #e6b89c (Numbers/constants)
- **Rose**: #d4c4bd (Parameters)

### Pastels

- **Mauve**: #b5a5d6 (Classes/namespaces)
- **Pink**: #d4a5c9 (Keywords/tags)
- **Red**: #d88a8a (Errors)
- **Maroon**: #d89999 (Secondary errors)

---

**Theme Philosophy**: Wintergreen combines the coolness of winter with the vitality of evergreen forests. The palette uses muted, frosty blues and greens as its foundation, with carefully selected pastel accents that provide warmth without overwhelming the cool aesthetic.
